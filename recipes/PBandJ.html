Recipe for PB&J

Peanut butter and jelly sandwhichs or, as they are usually referred to as, "PB&J" are a bread based delicacy featuring peanut butter, jelly and bread.

Ingrediants

  • bread
  • jelly
  • peanut butter

Steps

  1. place bread on a plate
  2. apply jelly
  3. place second slice of bread on plate
  4. apply peanut butter
  5. place the two pieces of bread together
  6. eat
